Need Assistance?

Follow this quick guide to sign in or create a new account.

How to Sign In

  • Go to the Sign in here form on the main page.
  • Enter your registered Registration ID / email / phone in the first field.
  • Type your Password. Use the eye icon to check the password if needed.
  • Click Sign in. If your details are correct, you will be redirected to your dashboard.
  • If you have forgotten your password, select Forgot password? to reset it.

How to Create a New Account

  • On the main sign-in page, choose the Sign up link.
  • Fill in your Full Name, Email, and a strong Password.
  • Select your Role (Student, Staff, or Admin). Additional fields appear when required.
  • Click Sign Up. A success message confirms your account.
  • You will be redirected back to the sign-in screen to log in with your new credentials.

Troubleshooting Tips

  • Ensure the email or registration ID matches exactly what was used during sign-up.
  • Passwords are case-sensitive. Check your keyboard layout and Caps Lock.
  • Still facing issues? Reach your system administrator or email support@chanakyauniversity.edu.in.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my role later? Contact support to request role updates.

Why didn’t I get a confirmation email? Check your spam folder. If the email doesn’t arrive within 10 minutes, contact support.

What if I don’t know my Registration ID? Use your registered email or get in touch with your department admin.
